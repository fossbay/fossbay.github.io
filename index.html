Fossbay

Simple, light, and free open-source software.


What is Fossbay?

Fossbay is a software development organization focused on building light and easy to use software for the public.

We also provide simple developer libraries like our cutils projects.

What we do

Fossbay was formed to make software development easier and more accessible, bringing down the barrier of entry for those who may find it too hard or daunting. Computer science is for everyone.

You can find some of our projects below, with links to their source code.

  • Easymake - A simple and fast build system.
  • cutils - A library to extend and simplify C development
  • ucc - The Micro C Compiler

Like what you hear?

You can join our Discord (link at the top of page) Feel free to join and hang out, or even contribute to these projects!

If you'd like to learn more, you can click on the GitHub button at the top of the page. All projects are well documented and have a wiki, if needed.
